Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Who We Are

RelaxRelease is operated by Siddharth Kundu, an individual based in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile picture obtained from GitHub or Google OAuth login
  • GitHub data: Repository names, commit messages, tags, and webhook events from repositories you connect
  • Billing information: Plan type and payment status (payment details are processed by Lemon Squeezy and never stored by us)
  • Usage data: Release history, release notes generated, and timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Generate release notes from your repository data
  • Send release notification emails (if enabled)
  • Manage your billing plan and access limits
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. GitHub Access

When you connect a GitHub repository, we request OAuth permissions to install webhooks and read repository metadata. We only access repositories you explicitly connect. We do not access your private code beyond what is necessary to generate release notes from commit messages and tags.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on infrastructure located in the United States. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Service:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database
  • Google Gemini — AI generation of release notes
  • Lemon Squeezy — Payment processing
  • GitHub — OAuth login and webhook integration

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

7. Cookies

We use HTTP-only cookies to maintain your authenticated session. These cookies are essential for the Service to function and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising cookies.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, HTTP-only secure cookies, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Disconnect your GitHub repositories at any time from the dashboard

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
